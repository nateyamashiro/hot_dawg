# Discovery Questions — Steal a Glizzy 🌭

> **Purpose:** the question bank the next session works through with Nate to define exactly what
> game we're building. Ask conversationally in small batches (2–4 at a time), not all at once.
> Record answers in `GAME_DESIGN.md`. Skip anything already locked (see top of that file).
>
> Legend: 🎯 = high-priority (answer early, it unblocks Milestone 2). ⭐ = shapes retention/virality.

---

## 1. Core fantasy & hook

- 🎯 In one sentence, what does the player *do* and *feel*? ("I hoard absurd hot dogs and raid
  my friends' stands.")
- What's the single clip a player would post to TikTok? (Design toward making that moment happen.)
- What emotion is the game selling — greed, chaos, mastery, collection, social flex?
- Is the tone wholesome-silly, chaotic-competitive, or edgy-funny? (Affects all copy/art.)

## 2. The steal-and-defend loop ⭐ (this is the retention engine)

- 🎯 How does stealing physically work — walk up and hold a prompt? Carry the dog back to your
  base? A timed minigame?
- 🎯 While carrying a stolen dog, what's the risk? (Slowed? Visible marker? Owner alerted?
  Can others steal it mid-carry?)
- 🎯 How does an owner *defend*? (Presence required? Traps? Walls? Guards you buy? Auto-locks?)
- Can you steal while offline-owner? i.e., are unattended bases raidable? How protected?
- What happens to a stolen dog — permanent transfer, or does the victim get a chance to steal back?
- Is there PvP combat (weapons to knock out thieves), or is it non-violent tag/chase only?
- 🎯 Is theft the *primary* way to get rare dogs, or a side activity on top of cooking/buying?
- Cooldowns/limits on stealing to prevent griefing spawn-camping?
- Should there be a "safe" mode/newbie protection window?

## 3. Bases / plots

- 🎯 Does each player get a fixed plot on a shared map, or instanced/personal bases?
- How many hot dogs can a base display/hold? Does that cap upgrade?
- What does the base look like — a hot dog stand, a food truck, a restaurant, a factory?
- Are dogs shown on pedestals/grills (stealable objects) or abstract (just a number)?
- Can players decorate/customize their stand? (Cosmetic monetization hook.)

## 4. Economy & progression

- 🎯 What's the primary currency? Just coins, or coins + a premium/prestige currency?
- What do coins buy — more cook rolls, base upgrades, storage slots, better grills, defenses?
- 🎯 Is there a **rebirth/prestige** loop (reset for a permanent multiplier)? What resets, what persists?
- How long should the first session be before a player feels progress? (First rare? First upgrade?)
- What are the deliberate "walls" that create the next goal? (Slot cap, cost curve, rarity gate.)
- Offline earnings — do dogs generate coins while you're away? Capped?

## 5. Hot dog collection & rarity

- Keep 7 tiers (Common→Secret) or change the ladder?
- 🎯 How big should the roster get at launch, and over time? (Collection = retention.)
- Should some dogs be **limited/event-only** (FOMO), or all always-available?
- Do we want a visible "dex"/collection UI showing what you've found vs. missing?
- Do duplicates matter (fusion/combine to upgrade?) or just stack as count?
- Any "mutations"/variants (Gold, Rainbow, Giant) on top of base dogs? (Multiplies content cheaply.)
- Naming style — pure food puns, meme-y, or a mix? Any characters/lore per dog?

## 6. The cook / gacha system (coin-only, locked)

- 🎯 Flat cook cost or scaling? Should better "grills" improve odds or unlock higher tiers?
- Single roll only, or multi-roll (cook 10)? Any pity system (guaranteed rare after N)?
- Should odds be **disclosed** in-UI? (Good practice + trust, even for coin-only.)
- Is cooking the only acquisition, or also a conveyor/shop of specific dogs to buy directly?

## 7. Map & world

- One big shared server map, or hub + separate raid areas?
- Target players per server (affects steal dynamics)? 10? 20? 50?
- Theme of the world — city food court, beach boardwalk, stadium, surreal glizzy-land?
- Any environmental mechanics (day/night, rush events, roaming NPC thieves)?

## 8. Retention systems ⭐

- 🎯 Daily login rewards / daily missions — what cadence and reward types?
- Limited-time events (weekly)? Themed hot dogs, leaderboards, seasonal maps?
- Quests/achievements with milestone rewards?
- A battle-pass-style track (free + premium lanes)?
- Global/friends leaderboards — by coins, rarest dog, most steals?
- Codes system (creators drop codes → installs)? Common growth lever on Roblox.

## 9. Social & multiplayer

- Friends/parties — team up to raid or defend together?
- Trading dogs between players? (Big engagement + economy risk — scams, dupes.)
- Chat/emotes/taunts? (Social drama fuels the "steal" fantasy.)
- Alliances/clans later?

## 10. Onboarding — the first 60 seconds

- 🎯 What's the very first thing a new player sees and does? (Must hook fast.)
- Guided tutorial, or learn-by-doing with tooltips?
- How fast do they get their first dog and their first steal opportunity?

## 11. Monetization (passes/products OK; gacha stays coin-only)

- Which **game passes**? (2× coins, extra base slots, auto-collect, VIP area, faster cook?)
- Which **dev products** (consumable Robux buys)? (Coin packs, instant refill, name change?)
- Cosmetics — skins for your stand, trails, titles, pets?
- Anything that must stay *earnable* free to keep it fair / non-pay-to-win?
- Comfortable with a "2× coins" pass given "no paid gacha"? Where's the pay line?

## 12. Fairness & anti-exploit

- How aggressively should we guard against exploiters (dupe dogs, coin injection)?
- Anti-griefing rules for stealing (limits, cooldowns, protections) — reconfirm from §2.
- Report/mute needs?

## 13. Art & audio direction (AI-generated art, locked)

- Visual style — blocky/voxel, smooth cartoon, realistic-ish food? (Prompt style for AI gen.)
- Color palette / vibe (bright & poppy vs. neon chaos)?
- Music & SFX direction — goofy, hype, chiptune, licensed-sounding?
- UI aesthetic — clean modern, sticker-bomb meme, retro?

## 14. Branding & meta

- 🎯 Final game name — keep "Steal a Glizzy" or alternatives? (Check name availability/moderation.)
- Thumbnail/icon concept (huge factor in Roblox click-through)?
- Social presence — TikTok/YT for the game to drive installs?

## 15. Scope & release plan

- 🎯 What's the **minimum** feature set for a first public release (soft launch)?
- Target date / how many hours per week can Nate put in?
- Solo dev or will others join? (Affects branching/PR workflow.)
- Playtest plan — friends first, then public? Roblox's built-in analytics goals?

---

### How to use this doc

Move each answered item into `GAME_DESIGN.md` under its section, then tick it here or delete it.
Prioritize the 🎯 items — they unblock Milestone 2 (the stealing layer). The ⭐ items determine
whether the game actually retains players, so give them real attention even though they're
"later" features.
